The Battle of Your Life

What Is This Game About?

This game is based on a real life event that occured during the year of 1940. This is named, the Battle of Britain. The Battle of Britain is a series of air attacks conducted by the German Luftwaffe to gain air superiorty against the British. But all of it backfired as Adolf Hitler underestimated the power of the RAF. This story/game is based on a British soldier that is relaxing by the brand new radar towers. Then they were suddenly surpised by the attack of the German Stukas that are trying to disable the radar tower. Now it depends on YOU to help the soldier survive.
